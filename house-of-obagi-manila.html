27. At 60 kilometers per hour L ittakes Loida 5 hours to travelfrom Manila to their house inTarlac. How long it take her ifshe travels at 50 kilometersper hour? Solves problem involving variation.​


Answer:

6 Hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Equation:  [tex]L=\frac{k}{hours}[/tex]

Use these values: L = 60, hours = 5, to solve for k:

[tex]60=\frac{k}{5} \\(5)(6)=\frac{k}{5}(5)\\k = 300[/tex]

Use these values: L = 50, k = 300, to solve for hours:

[tex]50=\frac{300}{hours}\\(hours)(50)=\frac{300}{hours}(hours)\\(50)(hours)=300\\\frac{(50)(hours)}{50} = \frac{300}{50}\\hours = 6[/tex]
