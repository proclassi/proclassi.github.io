9. Kayla walked 2 feet on the balance beam before she fell. Mackenzie walked 5 times as far before she fell. How far did Mackenzie walk on the balance beam before she fell?


Answer:

If Mackenzie walked 5 times farther than Kayla, then we can find how far Mackenzie walked by multiplying Kayla's distance by 5.

Kayla walked 2 feet, so:

5 x 2 feet = 10 feet

Therefore, Mackenzie walked 10 feet on the balance beam before she fell.
