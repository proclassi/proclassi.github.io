Because of the way the table was constructed (by doubling every time a new row is generate), the left hand column describes what has Happened to the Column on the right:

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \begin{gathered} \: \begin{gathered} \begin{array}{|c|c|c|}\hline \sf{}&\sf{1}&\sf{ \: 24 = 1 \times 24}\\ \hline \tt{}&\tt{{2}}&\tt{{ \: 48 = 2 \times 24}} \\ \hline \tt{}& \tt{{4}}& \tt{{ \: 96 = 4 \times 24}} \\ \hline \tt{}& \tt{{8}} & \tt{{ \: 192 = 8 \times 24}} \\ \hline \tt& \tt{{16}} &\tt{{ \: 384 = 16 \times 24}} \\ \hline\end{array}\end{gathered}\end{gathered}[/tex]

∴Therefore, [tex]24 + 96 + 192 = 1 \times 24 + 4 \times 24 + 8 \times 24.[/tex]
