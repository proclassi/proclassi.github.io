28. why do you think that those people should be your your target people in chess tournament


Chess Tournaments help a child fulfill intermediate chess goals. Winning games in the classroom or at a friend's house is fun, but when one wins a tournament game it is memorable and downright invigorating. The losses can be devastating if you don't prepare a child. We always stress that you will win and lose in chess.
