The transferability of these skills beyond the gaming context can help technology students in their studies. Consequently, the usage of mobile games can enable technology-vocational students to understand various concepts and tasks better than non-gamers.

In conclusion, mobile games can have both positive and negative impacts on the academic performance of technology-vocational students at VMUF.
