13. The pope says the galileo conflict led to a tragic mutual incomprehension what was the nature of thid mutual misunderstandingpasagot po ng maayos thank you​.


Answer:

The nature of the mutual misunderstanding between Galileo Galilei and the Catholic Church in the Galileo affair was that each side believed that the other was acting in bad faith and seeking to suppress the truth. The Church viewed Galileo's heliocentric ideas as heretical and opposed to the teachings of the Bible, while Galileo believed that his ideas were supported by scientific evidence and that the Church was hindering the progress of science. This led to a tragic conflict, as each side believed that it was acting in the pursuit of truth and the other was attempting to suppress it. The conflict ultimately resulted in Galileo being tried and found guilty of heresy by the Inquisition, and he spent the rest of his life under house arrest.
