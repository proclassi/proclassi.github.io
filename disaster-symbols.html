2. plssss help me my activity Think and illustrate a symbol that will represent how disaster risk reduction is important in your life. Then, discuss briefly why you use the symbol to represent the importance of DRR.​


One symbol that represents the importance of Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) in my life is a life buoy. A life buoy is a device used to rescue people who are in danger of drowning or struggling in water.

I chose the life buoy as a symbol of DRR because it signifies safety and preparedness. Just like a life buoy, DRR measures aim to mitigate the impact of disasters and protect individuals and communities from harm.

In general, disasters can strike at any time, and being prepared can make a significant difference in reducing the damage and saving lives. The life buoy reminds us of the importance of being prepared for emergencies and taking steps to reduce the risks of disasters.

Overall, the life buoy symbolizes the essential role that DRR plays in our lives, emphasizing the importance of preparedness and the need to take proactive steps to protect ourselves and our communities from disaster.
