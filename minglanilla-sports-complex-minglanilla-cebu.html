5 Festivals in Cebu

Type of Festival: Religious

2. Kadaugan sa Mactan

Description: Kadaugan sa Mactan (the Victory in Mactan) festival is organized to honor Lapu-Lapu chieftain and his triumph over Magellan that dates back to 16th century.

Place of origin: Lapu-lapu City

Type of Festival: Secular

Description: Palawod Festival is an annual celebration of the municipality of Bantayan depicts the daily life of the fishermen in the island.

Place of origin: Bantayan Island, Cebu

Type of Festival: Secular

4. Karansa Festival

Description: Karansa Festival is a popular merry-making celebration among the inhabitants of Danao, Cebu Province. The festivity includes various dancing performances, eating and drinking in celebration to the area's pottery-making livelihood.

Place of origin: Danao, Cebu Province

Type of Festival: Secular

5. Sinanggiyaw Festival

Description: Sinanggiyaw Festival is a fun celebration being held every 4th of October in Dumanjug, Cebu, basically features a festive mood showcasing the fertility of its land and abundance of the town's farm products.

Place of origin: Dumanjug, Cebu

Type of Festival: Secular

16. At a sports complex, the number of children, men, and women who are inside in the ratio 2:3:5. If 25 000 people are inside the sports complex, how many are women? ​


Answer:12,500

Step-by-step explanation:2n+3n+5n=10n

10n=25,000

25,000/10=2,500

2n=2(2,500)=5,000 children

3n=3(2,500)=7,500 men

5n=5(2,500)=12,500 women

Checking:

5,000+7,500+12,500=25,000
