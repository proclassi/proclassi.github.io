The rate of discount is 15%

D= (L−S)/L × 100

where,

L = List Price

S = Sale Price

D = Discount percentage

D= (L−S)/L × 100

D= (15,999−13,599.15)/15,999 × 100

D= 2399.85/15,999 × 100

D= 0.15 × 100

D= 15
