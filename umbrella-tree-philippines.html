20. house : roof :: _________ A.) feet : socks B.) head : cap C.) rain : umbrella D.) spoon : fork E.) tree : shade


ANALOGYAnalogy- This is comparing the opposites using the symbol (:). This is comparison is used to get the meaning of words or explain hard terms by comparing them to their opposites or something that is paired by a given object.

house: roof:  spoon : fork

a.) feet : socks - This is not an analogy because feet need socks.

b.) head : cap- This is not an analogy too because a head is not the other way        

                        around of the cap.

c. ) rain : umbrella- The same as with letters a and b.

d. ) soon : fork- This is the answer because the spoon is the opposite thing

                        for the fork.

e.) tree: shade- This too is not the answer because a tree naturally has a shade.

Other examples:

hot: cold

yes: no

summer : winter

black : white

Answer:

B.) head : cap

That is an example of analogy question. Analogy is the comparing of two different objects which have something in common or relevance to other. When there is an analogy question, another example is usually given but its counterpart or the other word is not. You can only find out the answer by finding the clues.

(Just like ratio and proportion in mathematics, both sides have something in common.)

On the question,

House : roof :: _________

The clue that I noticed is that the first word (house) is a thing and the second one (roof) is a part of it.

Another one is that the second word (roof) can be found on top of the first word (house).

Lastly, the roof also covers part of the house.

The clues collected will be the criteria or basis on finding the answer.

Looking at the sets of possible answers, it is clear that choice B is the only one that closely matches with the given clues. Although it is not part of the head, the cap can be seen on top of the head. And, it also covers a part of it. Choice A can also be the possible answer as sock covers feet but it only fills one criteria unlike Choice B. So, Choice B is the best answer.
