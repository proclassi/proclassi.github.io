27. 10. The total number of freshmen in the Philippine Science High School is 240. If 40% of thestudents came from private schools and 5s of these are boys, what is the ratio of girls belongingto private schools to the number of students from public schools?​


Answer:

5:4

Step-by-step explanation:

40% of the students came from private schools

= 240(40%)

= 240(2/5)

= 28(2) = 56

56 are from private schools

⅝ of these are boys

= 56(⅝)

= 7(5) = 35

If there 35 boys, then there are 240-56=184 girls

Then, the ratio is

⇒ 240:184

= 120:92

= 60:48

= 5:4
