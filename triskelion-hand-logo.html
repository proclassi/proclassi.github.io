12. A good hand drawn logo design start with _____ before executing the design in a more accurate manner.


Answer

A good hand-drawn logo design starts with SKETCHES OF THE STYLE YOU MAY WANT TO DO before executing the design in a more accurate manner.

Explanation

In the art industry, a series of sketches of designs to choose from is called a STUDY or STUDIES. A professional artist would usually make 20-40 studies but would present at least three finalized studies to the client. The purpose of making studies is to come up with designs in a short time. Studies also help an artist create a solid design that is also feasible for marketing and brand identity.
