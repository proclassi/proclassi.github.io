4. What is the relevance of environmental science in Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education?​


answer:

Bachelor of Technical Teacher Education is a four-year ladderized degree program that provides students with the knowledge and skills to teach technical-vocational courses like Automotive, Electrical, Civil and Drafting technology, Food and Service Management and many more. It aims to equip students with a strong theoretical understanding of teaching and technology. The Bachelor of Technical Teacher Education program inculcates in students a deep understanding of the teaching and learning principles and theories needed in teaching technical-vocational courses. It also discusses how the educational processes relate to larger historical, social, cultural and political processes.

16. What is formal education, elementary education, secondary education, tertiary education, vocational education, non formal education and special education?


Hey dear

So your answer is

•Formal education - It is a kind of education normally delivered by trained teachers in a systematic intentional way within a school, higher education and University.

•Elementary education - it is typically the first stage of formal education coming after preschool and before •Secondary Education.

Secondary education - it is the final and second basic phase of the education which comes before tertiary education.

•Tertiary education - it is commonly referred to as post Secondary Education referred to academic persuits undertaken after High School.

•Vocational education - it is a kind of education that prepares people to work in various jobs, such as trade ,craft or as technician.

•Non formal education - non formal education is about acknowledging the importance of education, learning and training which takes place outside a recognised institution.

•Special education - it is a practice of educating students in a way that addresses their individual differences and needs.
