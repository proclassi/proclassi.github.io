7. ❖ The significance of the study discusses the purpose that the research will serve to society, the country, the government, the institution or agency concerned, the curriculum planners and developers, and the research community. explanation?


Answer:

The significance of the study is an important aspect of research, as it outlines the purpose that the research will serve to the various stakeholders involved. This includes society, the country, the government, the institution or agency concerned, the curriculum planners and developers, and the research community. By understanding the significance of the study, all of these stakeholders can better comprehend the value of the research and its potential impact. This understanding can then be used to inform decisions and actions related to the research.
