9. AG LINER BUS TRAVELS CROSSING EDSA ORTIGAS CHANGES ITS SPEED FROM 6M/S TO 12M/S IN 30 SEC. CALCULATE ITS ACCELERATION


Answer:A boat carries an initial speed of 6 m / s to the south, and at 4 seconds its speed is 12 m / s also to the south. Calculate: a. Its average acceleration. His displacement in that time.

The data you have are:

vi = 6 m / s

vf = 12 m / s

t = 4s

a =?

d =?

We calculate the acceleration.

a = (vf - vi) / t

a = (12 m / s - 6 m / s) / 4s

a = (6 m / s) / 4s

a = 1.5 m / s² ------------ Answer

We calculate the distance.

d = (vf + vi) / 2 * t

d = (12m / s + 6m / s) / 2 * 4s

d = (18 m / s) / 2 * 4s

d = 9m * 4

d = 36 m ------------ Answer
