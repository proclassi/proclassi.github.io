19. What are the opportunities brought about by globalization to law enforcement?


Opportunities brought by globalization for law enforcement.

As we know, globalization provides easy access to information from various parts of the world, one of which is in the field of law enforcement. How?

The ease of access to information allows law enforcers in a country to study various cases from abroad in handling legal cases that may rarely occur in their country. In addition, the law enforces can also look at various examples of judicial processes in other countries to help them find the most effective judicial methods for them to do.Globalization also allows lawmakers in one country to study various rules made in other countries. Like the effectiveness or lack of these rules, then innovating these rules into the most appropriate rules applied in their country to enforce the law.In addition, globalization allows law enforcers from various countries to establish connections. This will open a great opportunity for law enforcers to provide mutual assistance if needed.
