C. Php 16,768.00

(1,310*400)*3.2%=16,768Problem:Rose bought 400 shares of SBC stock at its 52-week low, Php 781.20 per share, and sold at 52-week high, Php 1,310,00 per share. Find the broker's commission if the broker charges 3.2% of the total sale price

a. Php 61,786.00

b. Php 61,768,00

c. Php 16,786.00

d. Php 16,768.00​

Solution:

Since the question is only asking for the commission of the total sale price,

Total shares bought = 400 shares of SBC stock

Selling price = 1310 php

charges = 3.2% = 3.2/100 = 0.032

Brokers commission = Total shares bought x Selling price x charges

Brokers commission = 400(1,310)(0.032)

Brokers commission = 16, 768 php
