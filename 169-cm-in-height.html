24. A piece of wire is shaped to enclose a square whose area is 169 cm2. It is then reshaped to enclose a rectangle whose length is 15 cm. What is the area of the rectangle?


Answer:

The rectangle has an area of 165 square centimeters.

Step-by-step explanation:

The problem deals with two areas. The area of the square and the area of the rectangle. The area of the square is solved by getting the square of its side. The area of the rectangle is solved by multiplying its length by its width.

Formulas of Areas

1. Square, [tex]A = s^2[/tex]

2. Rectangle, [tex]A= l\times{w}[/tex]

Given Details:

Square: [tex]A = 169\:\text{cm}^2[/tex]

Rectangle: [tex]l = 15\:\text{cm}[/tex]

Steps in Solving the Sides of the Square

1. Substitute the value of [tex]A=169[/tex] into the square area formula.

2. Take the square root of both sides.

3. Take the positive value since length can't be less than zero.

Solution:

                                      [tex]\begin{aligned}A&=s^2\\169&=s^2\\\pm\sqrt{169}&=\sqrt{s^2}\\\pm13&=s\\13&=s\end{aligned}[/tex]

This means that to form the piece of wire into a square, it should have a length of 13 cm each side.

Now, the rectangle has a length of 15 cm. Basically, we are adding 2 cm on two opposite sides of the square to reach the 15 cm. We take this 2 cm from the other two opposite sides of the square, so we have 11 cm left.

So, the rectangle has a length of 15 cm and a width of 11 cm. To solve for the area of the rectangle, substitute [tex]l=15[/tex] and [tex]w=11[/tex] into the rectangle area formula, then simplify.

Solution:

                                            [tex]\begin{aligned}A&=l\times{w}\\A&=15\:\text{cm}\times 11\:\text{cm}\\A&=165\:\text{cm}^2\end{aligned}[/tex]

This means that the rectangle has an area of [tex]165\:\text{cm}^2[/tex].

30. 1. A researcher collected the following height (in cm) of 15 students for his research: 163. 170, 155, 141, 165, 172, 120, 168, 164, 169, 172, 150, 163, 169, and 148. What is the mean height of the students?​


Answer:

159.27cm

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the mean of a set of numbers, just divide the sum of the numbers with how many numbers is in the set.

In your case,

n = 15 (numbers given in a set)

s = (163 + 170 + 155 + 141 + 165 + 172 + 120 + 168 + 164 + 169 + 172 + 150 + 163 + 169 + 148)

s = 2389

Now divide s by n, 2389 / 15 = 159.27cm.
