23. A bus left a bus station at 5:00pm and travels at the rate of 50kph. Another bus on the same route left the bus station 1 hour later and overtakes the first bus at 7:00pm. What is the rate of the second bus? with solution po please :)


Answer:

The 1st bus left the station and traveled for two hours, and the speed rate of the bus is 50kph.

Let S1 be the speed of the first bus, and S2 be the speed of 2nd bus.

Let's solve the distance traveled by the 1st bus:

Distance = Speed * time

Distance = S1 * Time = 50kph * 2h = 100kph

The second bus overtook the first bus at the time that the first bus traveled 100 km.

Let's solve the speed of the second bus:

2nd bus = distance/travel of time

              = 100 km/1 hour

Thus, the rate of the second bus is 100km per hour (100km/h)
