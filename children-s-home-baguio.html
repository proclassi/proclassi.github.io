Construct a function (s) where s is the speed of travel, that describes the time it takes to drive from Pampanga to Baguio


Answer:

10 hours if the speed is 25 km per hour 6 hours and 15 minutes if the speed is 40 km per hour 5 hours if the speed is 50 km per hour, and y= 250/s or f(s)= 250/s best describes for function.

Step-by-step explanation:

In mathematics, function define as a relation that associates for each element of a set x (domain of the function), to a single element of a set y (range of the function). In every input of value to x, there is corresponding value for an output to y.

Solution:

Let  

1. y is the number of hours traveled

2. s is the speed

For 25 kilometers per hour

y= 250 ÷ s  

y= 250 ÷ 25  

y= 10 hours

For 40 kilometers per hour

y= 250 ÷ s  

y= 250 ÷ 40  

y= 6.25 hours,  

0.25(60)= 15 minutes  

y = 6 hours and 15 minutes

For 50 kilometers per hour

y= 250 ÷ s  

y= 250 ÷ 50  

y= 5 hours  

For function (s)

y= 250 ÷ a  

y= 250/s  

or f(s) = 250/s
