29. how can you describe the corporate usage of intranet and extranets ​


Answer:

An intranet is a private - internal - business network that enables your employees to share information, collaborate, and improve their communications. An extranet enables your business to communicate and collaborate more effectively with selected business partners, suppliers and customers.

Intranet is typically used internally. While an extranet allows businesses to communicate with clients and vendors, an Intranet allows employees and colleagues to work with each other in a virtual space — no outside parties are involved.

The Corporation is a 2003 documentary film that examines the modern-day corporation and the impact it has on society. The film uses the concept of the corporation as a "psychopath" to highlight the negative aspects of corporate business practices. It provides a critical perspective on the contemporary corporation and its impact on society.

The film explores the legal and cultural origins of the corporation, its rise to prominence, and its impact on society and the environment. It also examines the role of corporations in shaping public policy and their influence on the media. The film includes interviews with a wide range of experts, including academics, business leaders, and activists.

Overall, The Corporation provides a thought-provoking look at the modern corporation and its impact on society. It offers a critical perspective on the corporation and its business practices, and serves as a valuable learning experience for anyone interested in the nature of large organizations.
