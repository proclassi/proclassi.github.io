2. The following table shows a proportional relationship between jjj and mmm. jjj mmm 444 333 121212 999 202020 151515 Write an equation to describe the relationship between jjj and mmm.


Answer:

The equation representing the proportional relationship between j and m is m = 3j/4 .

Step-by-step explanation:

Writing an Equation to Represent a Proportional Relationship

What is proportional relationship?

A proportional relationship between two quantities is when the two quantities vary directly with one and other. Meaning if one item is doubled, the other item is also doubled.

Going back to the given problem above, we have the table showing the proportional relationship between j and m.

j | 4 | 12 | 20 |

m | 3 | 9 | 15 |

Solution:

Step #1

The proportional relationship can be written as:

m/j = ¾ = ‌9⁄12‌ = ‌15⁄20‌ = ¾

Step #2

So, the equation representing this proportional relationship is:

m = ¾ × j/1 = 3j/4

Final Answer:

The equation representing the proportional relationship between j and m is m = 3j/4 .
