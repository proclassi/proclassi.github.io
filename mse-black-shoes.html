22. There are 48 pupils in the class. Five-eighths of the class are girls. One third of the girls and 1/2 of the boys have black shoes. How many pupils in the class have black shoes?


[tex]\tt{48\:×\:\frac{5}{8}\:=\:30}[/tex] are girls

Thus, [tex]\tt{48\:-\:30\:=\:18}[/tex] are boys

[tex]\tt{\frac{1}{3}\:×\:30\:=\:10}[/tex] girls wear black shoes

[tex]\tt{\frac{1}{2}\:×\:18\:=\:9}[/tex] boys wear black shoes

[tex]\tt{10\:+\:9\:=\:19}[/tex] students wear black shoes.
