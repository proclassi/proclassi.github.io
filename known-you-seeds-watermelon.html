3. How many seeds in Watermelon?


Answer:

Minimum = 0 (seedless)

Maximum = 150+

Step-by-step explanation:

If you want to know how many seeds are in the watermelon you have in front of you:

Weigh the watermelon

Cut off a good chunk, around 20% of the whole

Weigh the piece

Take out all seeds from the piece and count

You can even count mature seeds (black/brown) and immature seeds (white) separately

Estimate seeds in whole watermelon by multiplying no. of seeds in piece by ratio of whole weight to piece weight.

If you want to make it more accurate, first remove all the rind, then follow steps 1-6.

6. How many mongo seeds are equal to 4.50 moles of watermelon seeds?


Answer:

moles of mongo seed are -

Here,we have to use the Avogadro's number to calculate the total number of seeds.

According to the Avogadro's concept,

1 mole = 6.023 × 10²³ particles (or, seeds for this problem)

So, 4.5 moles = 4.5× 6.023 × 10²³ = 27.0315 × 10²³.

There are 27.0315×10²³ seeds in 4.5 moles of mongo seed.

(Although number of seeds are not measured by the mole unit, this problem is just used to demonstrate students about the basic functions of the Avogadro's concept in a simple way.)
