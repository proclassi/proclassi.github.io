9. what is hydraulic pipe bender?​


Answer:

Hydraulic pipe benders are mechanisms that bend various sizes of metal pipe and electrical conduit to specific angles using hydraulic pressure. They work in the same way that hydraulic automotive jacks do, with a hydraulic piston that raises to exert high pressure on the pipe between the bending dies. They can bend multiple times faster and with less effort than manual benders. Steel, aluminum, and brass pipe shaping models are available for a variety of applications ranging from automotive repair to bar bending for landscape and concrete work.

11. what is the main objective of pipe bender?​


Answer:

The main objective of a pipe bender is to bend the pipe without any buckling (collapsing under pressure), and with as little crimping and flattening as possible. It is important that the pipe keeps its shape as deformed pipes can break under pressure, crack and generally look less appealing.
