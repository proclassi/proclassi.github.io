5. An express train travels 150 km in the same time that a freight train travels 100 km. if the express train goes 20 km per hour faster than the freight train, find the rate of the freight train


Answer:

40 km/h

Step-by-step explanation:

150/x = 100/(x-20)

100x = 150(x-20)

100x = 150x - 3000

-50x = -3000

x = 60 km/h (speed of the express train)

-------

x-20 = 40 km/h (speed of the freight train)

8. a forwarding company charges P1,100 for the first 20 kg and P60 for each succeeding 2kg for freight sent to europe.how much do you need to pay for a box weighing 88lb?


Convert 88 lbs. to kgs.:

88 lbs ÷ 2.2 lbs/kg. = 40 kg.

1)First 20 kg = Php 1,100

2)Subtract the first 20 kg from total 40 kg:
  40 kg. - 20 kg = 20 kg, remaining kg

3) For every succeeding 2 kg, the cost is Php60.  Multiply by 20 remaining kg.
    (Php 60/2kg) x (20 kg)
    = (Php 30/kg) x (20 kg)
    =  Php 600

4)  Add the freight charges:
    Php 1,100 + Php 600 = Php 1,700, freight charges for 88 lbs.
