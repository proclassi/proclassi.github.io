4. Part Three: Sex-linked Inheritance We are down to just and performed to test for red-green color blindness. Mr. Quintana Mrs. Quintana Color blind Homozygous for normal vision So, who gets the money? The lawyers now order that a vision test be Manuel Isagani Color blind Normal vision (justify your answer)​


Answer:

The correct answer is Manuel Isagani.Since Manuel is the only person in the family who does not have a sex-linked disorder, he is the only one who can inherit the money.

Explanation:

Since Mr. and Mrs. Quintana are both colorblind, they both have a sex-linked disorder. This means that they will both pass on their disorder to their children. Since Manuel does not have a sex-linked disorder, he will not inherit it from his parents.
