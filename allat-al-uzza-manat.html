Decancing TI 3 Magnetism pararen erant 4 Futtering solids from iquid Sieving E pomaten tror attei from mon manatic 5.pa help naman po please​


ANSWERS:D.B.E.A.C.EXPLANATION:EVAPORATION:

- Evaporation is a process of separating mixtures which involves heating the solution until the solvent evaporates leaving behind the solid residue. Heat being the main component in this process separates mixtures of solid from a liquid. As liquid goes in the air in a form of gas when heated changing liquid to gas as an example of physical change.

DECANTING:

- Decantation or also known as (Decanting) is a technique used in separating a less dense substance from a denser one. In the process of decantation, the mixture is left undisturbed. When the components are separated, the less than substance can be removed by scooping using a spoon or it can be removed by slowly pouring out the less dense substance. Example are Oil in water, gasoline in water, sinigang with floating fats, muf and water.

MAGNETISM:

- Magnetism or also known as Use of magnets, components of mixtures can be separated in many ways. The method that is being use usually depends on the type of mixture. Metal and non-metal objects can be separated by using a magnet. There is no chemical reaction involved in the separation of components. Metal which are made up alloy, nickel or cobalt are easily attracted to the magnet, while non-metals are not. Iron fillings, thumb tacks, staple wire, pins, coins, and needle arr some common examples of objects that are attracted to the magnet. When they mixed with non-metals, these could be separated by magnet.

FILTERING:

- Solid and liquid components can be separated with the use of filter paper or filter cloth as a medium in which liquid passes through a filter paper leaving insoluble solid material in the medium.

SIEVING:

- Sieving is as a method in which two or more components of different sizes are separated from a mixture on the basis of the difference in their sizes which uses a sieve.
