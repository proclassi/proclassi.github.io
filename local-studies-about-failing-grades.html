13. >What can you say about "Pass or Fail grading system" ?>Do you oppose this mechanism grading system?​


MY VIEWS ON PASS OR FAIL GRADING SYSTEM OF THE COUNTRY

Answer:

First of all, what is Pass or Fail Grading System is that instead of the traditional grading where you receive a letter grade of A to F, your grade becomes a binary — either you pass or you fail.

What Can I say?

I think, the Pass or Fail Grading System minimize the expectations of students nowadays. It has been proven that competition in classes help students to study harder. Students may feel that the system discourages them to study harder because it limits the opportunity for them to see that they are being appreciated. It also removes the chance for students to see which area they should focus or improve.

Am I Oppose?

However, there may also be an advantage for this mechanism of grading system. It may reduce the stress of students and avoid the comparing their capabilities to others students. Therefore I would not oppose such a system.
