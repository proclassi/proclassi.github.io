PROBLEM:

The strawberries in Bagiuo City is so cheap back in time, Orbits Bakery Shop stocks around 40 kg of strawberries a week back in 2019, because of the pandemic, the price went up to 400 php and the stock went down to 25kg. What do you think is the price of the strawberries in year 2019?

P (price) direct varies to S (stocks)

ANSWER:

To get the price of the strawberries in year 2019, let us use our knowledge in ratio and proportion. Since the price (p) varies directly as stocks (s), therefore they are directly proportional to each other.

Let x be the unknown value. We'll have the proportion:

[tex] \sf x:40=400:25[/tex]

In fraction form, we have:

[tex] \sf \frac{x}{40} = \frac{400}{25} \\ [/tex]

Performing the cross multiplication to solve for the value of x.

[tex] \sf 25x = 400(40)[/tex]

[tex] \sf 25x = 16000[/tex]

[tex] \sf \frac{25x}{25} = \frac{16000}{25} \\ [/tex]

[tex] \sf x = 640[/tex]

Therefore, the price of the strawberries in year 2019 is Php. 640.00.

As the price of the strawberries increases, the stocks increases. In the problem's case, the stocks decreases with regards to the price.

18. A homozygous white horse is crossed with homozygous red horse and resulted to an offspring with roan phenotype. Which of the following pattern of inheritance is exhibited? a. Incomplete Dominance b. Segregation c. Codominance d. Sex related inheritance​


Question

A homozygous white horse is crossed with homozygous red horse and resulted to an offspring with roan phenotype. Which of the following pattern of inheritance is exhibited?

A. Incomplete Dominance

B. Segregation

C. Codominance

D. Sex related inheritance​

Answer

C. Codominance

In shorthorn cattle, when a homozygous white horse (WW) is crossed with a  homozygous red horse (RR), all the offspring are roan-a spotted, white and red or red milky color. When a homozygous red horse is bred with a homozygous white horse? The white and red hairs in the coat of the offspring that is made by homozygous red horse and a homozygous white horse is an example of codominance.

CONDOMINACE:

Codominance means that neither allele can mask the expression of the other allele. An example in humans would be the ABO blood group, where alleles A and alleles B are both expressed. So if an individual inherits allele A from their mother and allele B from their father, they have blood type AB.
