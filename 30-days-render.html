7. what is color rendering​


A color rendering index is a quantitative measure of the ability of a light source to reveal the colors of various objects faithfully in comparison with an ideal or natural light source. Light sources with a high CRI are desirable in color-critical applications such as neonatal care and art restoration.

29. Caroline was rendered speechless to such a degree that she talked of nothing else for the rest of the day a. irony b. hyperbole c. personification d. euphemism ​


ANSWER: A

EXPLANTATION: Irony is a figure of speech where the words used contradict what they actually mean. In simple words, it seems like sarcastic remarks. In this statement, it was stated that Caroline was rendered speechless when, in fact, it actually means that she didn't stop talking for the rest of the day.
