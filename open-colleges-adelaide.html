26. Ashley is the employee of the month at her job.her reward is to select at random from 4 gift cards, 6 coffee mugs, 7DVDs,10 picture frames, and 3 gift baskets. what is the probability that Adelaide receives a gift card, coffee mug, or picture frame?​


There are 20 gift cards, coffee mugs, and picture frames that Ashley can choose from. The total number of possible gifts that Ashley can select from is 30 So the probability is 2/3 or approximately 0.667.

Explanation

Ashley is randomly selecting a gift from a total of 4 gift cards, 6 coffee mugs, 7 DVDs, 10 picture frames, and 3 gift baskets.

The total number of possible gifts that Ashley can select from is the sum of all the individual items, which is:

4 + 6 + 7 + 10 + 3 = 30

The number of gift cards, coffee mugs, and picture frames that Ashley can select from is the sum of the individual items in these categories, which is:

4 + 6 + 10 = 20

Therefore, the probability that Ashley receives a gift card, coffee mug, or picture frame is:

(number of gift cards, coffee mugs, and picture frames) / (total number of possible gifts)

              = 20 / 30

              = 2 / 3

So the probability that Ashley receives a gift card, coffee mug, or picture frame is 2/3 or approximately 0.667.
