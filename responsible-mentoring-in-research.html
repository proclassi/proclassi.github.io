15. who's of Plato's mentor?​


Answer:

Plato's mentor was Socrates. Socrates was a Greek philosopher who lived in Athens during the 5th century BCE. He is known for his method of questioning, which is now known as the Socratic method, and for his belief in the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom through self-examination and critical thinking.

Plato was a student of Socrates and was greatly influenced by his teachings. After Socrates' execution in 399 BCE, Plato founded the Academy in Athens, which became one of the most important centers of philosophical and scientific learning in the ancient world. Plato's philosophy was heavily influenced by Socrates, and he often used Socrates as a character in his dialogues to explore his own philosophical ideas.
