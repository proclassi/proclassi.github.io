1. When the schedule of ranking for teacher for 2018 -2019?


The Department of Education issued a memorandum already to all Schools Division regarding Preparation of the Registry of Qualified Applicants (RQA) for Teachers on Elementary and Secondary High School for SY: 2018-2019.

In every Division office has own schedule:
In the Division of Taguig City the following is the schedule:
       January 5 - 12, 2018, Acceptance of Pertinent papers in all public schools.
        January 15, 2018, Verification as to completeness of documents.
        January 26, 2018, Orientation of Teachers Applicants.
        March 3, 2018, Conduct of English Proficiency examination.
        March 19 - 30, Consolidated of Ranking, Demonstration Teaching, and interview. date to be announced.
        April 2 - 6, 2018 Finalization of RQA.
        April 13, 2018- Release of RQA 

Note: In every division office, the schedule may vary.

3. 4. Perform regular maintenance schedule.​


Answer:

Scheduled maintenance is any repair and upkeep work performed within a set timeframe. It details when given maintenance tasks are performed and by whom. Scheduled maintenance may occur at repeating intervals or in response to a work request.Scheduled maintenance includes inspections, adjustments, regular service, and planned shutdowns. An example of scheduled maintenance occuring at repeating intervals is changing a bearing on a conveyor belt every 30 days or inspecting the condition of a motor every 90 days.
