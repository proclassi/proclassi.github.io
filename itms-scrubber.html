5. what is the use of dishcloths,scrubbers, sponges,steelwool​


Answer:

Cleaning

Explanation:

All of those are used to clean

Dishcloths or dishrags are used in the kitchen to clean dishes and surfaces.

Scrubbers or Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) are used to remove particulate matter and harmful components, such as sulphur oxides (SOx) and nitrogen oxides (NOx) from the exhaust gasses generated as a result of combustion processes in marine engines, to implement pollution control.

Sponges are tools or cleaning aids made of soft, porous material. Typically used for cleaning impervious surfaces, sponges are especially good at absorbing water and water-based solutions.

Steel wool is used as an abrasive in finishing and repair work for polishing wood or metal objects, cleaning household cookware, cleaning windows, and sanding surfaces.
