Then lift up on to the toes on your standing leg and flex your knee and ankle slightly so you can hop up and down on your standing leg for the required duration.

The benefits of jumping include improved cardiovascular health, metabolism, bone density, strength, muscle tone, balance and coordination. Regular exercise, good sleep and a healthy diet are important pillars for a healthy body
