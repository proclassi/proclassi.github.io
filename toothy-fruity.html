Basically, if it's concrete, you can touch/hear/smell/see the noun(it's basically physical)

Abstract is when you can't touch/hear/see/smell it, but you can feel and think about it if that made sense

Examples of concrete are everything you can see/touch/smell/hear

Examples of abstract are dreams, nightmares, anger, love, and etc.

Bold- Concrete

Italic and underlined- Abstract
