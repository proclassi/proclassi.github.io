4. What is the history of Barangay Santiago I in San Pablo City?


Answer:

San Pablo City's earliest historical record dates back to pre-Spanish times when four large barrios at the foot of Mount Makiling bounded together and called the place "Sampalok". In 1571, the first Spanish troops under Captain Juan de Salcedo arrived in the village, which was transformed into a parish in 1586.

Answer:

History has it that the name Laoag came from the Ilocano word meaning light, clarity or brightness. Laoag City is 450 km north of Manila
