19. How does the aesthetic view affect the style and designs of native crafts, accessories, and ornaments?​


Answer

Aesthetics is the study of beauty and art that is a part of axiology, which is the philosophy of studying what people like, or how people put value and evaluate things. It has many influences like education, community lifestyle, culture, belief, traditions, social changes, and other human experiences.

So, the aesthetics of the style and design of indigenous art and craftsmanship are heavily influenced by religious beliefs and traditions from animism. One example is the Ifugao Bul'ul with a ceremonial bowl called pamahan, which is used as an altar for offerings and protection.
