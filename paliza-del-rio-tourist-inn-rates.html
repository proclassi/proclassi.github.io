27. Rio and kim had the same amount of money at the start. When rio gave 300 the ratio of rio's mobey to kim's money became 2:3. How much money they have each at first?


At the start = same amoun of money:

Rio's money:x                        Kim's money: x

After Rio gave 300 to Kim, the ratio becomes 2:3.

(x - 300) : (x + 300) = 2: 3

3(x - 300) = 2 (x + 300)

3x - 900 = 2x + 600


3x - 2x = 600 + 900

x = 1,500

Rio and Kim had 1,500 each.
