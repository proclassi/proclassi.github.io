Answer:SEE IMAGE

Explanation: (12-Hour Format)00:00 H is 12:00 AM. Such as 00:30 H is 12:30 AMKapag 01:00 H to 11:00 H, rewite lang yung time then change H to AM. Such as 04:30 H is 4:30 AM.Kapag 12:00 H, rewrite lang din then change H to PM. Such as 12:57 H is 12:57 PMKapag 13:00 H to 23:00 H, convert it into a 12-hour format by subtracting 12 to them and changing H to PM. Such as 16:00 H is (16-12=) 4:00 PM

Explanation: (24-Hour Format)Kapag 12:00 am po, ang 24-hour format niya is 00:00 H po.kung 01:00 AM to 11:00 AM, convert niyo lang siya to 24-hour format by rewriting it then change AM to H such as 2:30 AM is 02:30 HBy 12:00 PM, ganun din. Rewrite niyo lang siya then change PM to H. 12:45 PM is 12:45 HFor 01:00 PM to 11:00 PM naman po, add 12 lang para makuha 24-hour format such as 4:00 PM is (4+12=) 16:00 H
