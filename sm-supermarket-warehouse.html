24. Alden has (100×²-5×+3) pesos in his wallet and he spent (80×²-2×+9) pesos in buying foods in SM supermarket,how much money is left in his wallet?​


Answer:

Hence, Alden have a total of 20x² - 3x - 6 money left in his wallet

Step-by-step explanation:

Given :

Total money = (100x² - 5x + 3)

Money spent in foods = (80x² - 2x + 9)

Solving for the money left,

(100x² - 5x + 3) - (80x² - 2x + 9)100x² - 5x + 3 - 80x² - 2x + 9100x² - 80x² - 5x + 2x - 80x² + 3 - 920x² - 3x - 6

Therefore, 20x² - 3x - 6 money left
