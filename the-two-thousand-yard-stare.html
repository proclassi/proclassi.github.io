ANSWER: 23, 000 If the digit at the right side of the number that was rounded is 5 and above, you will add one to the digit that is rounded.The answer is 23,000 yards.

The digit on the thousand place is  2. The number on its right is 8 and 8 is higher than 5. Therefore, the digit on the thousand place, which is 2, will be rounded up to 3. The remaining digits on the right will be 0.
