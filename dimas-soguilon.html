6. A loaf of bread dimas-alang bakery in kapasigan has a volume of 15 cm and a mass of 450 g. What is the density of the bread?


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The density of an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{d = \frac{m}{V}}[/tex]

where:

d = density

m = mass

V = volume

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for

[tex]d = \dfrac{m}{V}[/tex]

[tex]d = \dfrac{\text{450 g}}{\text{15 cm}^{3}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{d = \text{30 g/}\text{cm}^{3}}[/tex]

Therefore, the density of the loaf of bread is 30 g/cm³.
