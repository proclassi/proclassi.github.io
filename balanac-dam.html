In an ecosystem, each organism has its role and purpose. When we introduce external factors such as too much carbon dioxide or methane, it destroys the balance of the ecosystem which in turn affects those who live in it. The result is global warming, water shortage, extinction of species, etc.


2. what is diffirence of action reaction forces and balanaced forces?​


Answer:

Balanced forces are equal and opposite forces that act on the same object. That's why they cancel out while Action-reaction forces are equal and opposite forces that act on different objects, so they don't cancel out. In fact, they often result in motion.

Answer:

6. how do you know when balanaced forces are acting on the object?​


Answer:

When two forces acting on an object are equal in size but act in opposite directions, we say that they are balanced forces .

If the forces on an object are balanced (or if there are no forces acting on it), this is what happens: a stationary object stays still.

2. Coordination: Practice catching and throwing objects, such as a ball or beanbag. You can also practice lateral movements, such as side shuffles, cariocas, and crossover steps.
