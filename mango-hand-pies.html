PROPORTION

》happens when two ratios are equal

PROBLEM

A recipe that will make 5 mango pies calls for 11 cups of flour. Write a proportion and solve it to find how many mangoe pies can be made with 33 cups of flour.

ANSWERThere are 15 mango pies which can be made with 33 cups of flour.

SOLUTION

Let x be the number of pies can be made with 33 cups of flour.

[tex]5:11 = x:33 \iff \frac{5}{11} = \frac{x}{33} \\ \\\bigg(\frac{5}{11} = \frac{x}{33} \bigg)(33) \\ \\ \frac{(33)(5)}{11} = x \\ \\ \bold{x = 15}[/tex]

Ratio

A ratio indicates the relative sizes of two quantities. It is a comparison that tells how many times one number contains another. You can express the ratio in three ways. It can be in a word form (m is to p), fraction form (m/p) and colon form (m:p). Just like a fraction, you can also simplify the ratio. To give the simplest form of the ratio, look for the greatest common factor of the numbers and divide them by it.
