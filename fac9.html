18. fact and opinion Write a short essay about Advantages of Face to Face Classes ​


Answer:

Face to face class can be more interactive for both student and teachers, and it can be easier to see the students' reaction when things are explained. It can also help to create better environment for students to ask questions and raise their hands when something needs to be clarified. In some cases, it can also be easier to keep the attention in face to face classes since it requires you to focus on the teacher and not something else.

Face to face classes have plenty of advantages over online education, but online classes have some benefits of their own.
