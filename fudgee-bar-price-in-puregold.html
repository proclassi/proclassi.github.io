5. Puregold is giving 10% discount to its loyal customers/retailers called “SUKI” discount. If the price of a sack of sugar they are selling is P995.00,how much trade discount will be received by the retailer if the order is 50 sacks​


Answer:

4,975

Step-by-step explanation:

995.00 is the price of a sack of sugar

10% discount to those mga suki

995.00 × 10% = 99.5

A retailer will buy 50 sacks.

Question is "How much trade discount will be received by the retailer?"

So...

99.5 × 50 = 4,975

The total discount is 4,975
