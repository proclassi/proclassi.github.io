1. similarities of cold storage and dry storage​


Answer:

Cold Storage means - storing food materials in a cold environment like a refrigerator. ... Dry Storage means - storing food materials in a place that is dry. It does not necessarily be cold, it can be at normal room temperature too, provided there is no humidity and moisture around.

The dry storage area is where stocks of cereals, rice, dried pasta, fruit and vegetables, tinned products and packaged foods are kept that do not require temperature control while the cold storage is a walk-in storage facility in controlled condition to keep a consistent temperature.
