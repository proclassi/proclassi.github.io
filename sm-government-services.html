PRICING STRATEGY OF SM MALL

Markdown- I believe the pricing strategy that the SM Malls should implement is Markdown in order to sell the merchandise that is not saleable.  Even though they will have low profit on their products, the good thing is they can still dispose  those products and at the same time help the other people to afford the products even if they have no source of income because some companies have closed due to lockdowns.

MARKDOWN PRICING STRATEGYThis strategy is cutting down the actual price of the product. In this strategy the the businessman used promotional strategy to enhance the sales of the product.People only purchase necessities during a pandemic; they do not purchase items that are not required for daily usage. As a result, SM Mall will have overstock product; as a result, the price of the product should be reduced. However, this does not imply that SM will sell the product at a loss.
