2. pledge of commitment to protect the environment​


Pledge of commitment to protect the environment

We hereby pledge to develop and uphold strict and long-term environmental principles, both individually and professionally. We pledge to be an example of how good living benefits both the community and the environment. We want to reduce, reuse, and recycle. We will contribute to and enable our city to reduce its carbon footprint. We will give back to our people and our planet in every way we can.

Additional:

Recycling Efforts:

We like recycling, and it shows. It all starts with our conscientious employees, who make recycling a part of their daily life by using our many recycling stations and saying "no" to paper cups and plastic dishes. We already have specifically numbered recycling bins for everyone in our shop to assist us in resolving this situation. What are cardboard boxes? Well, indeed! We also compost those. We also make it easy for you to contribute by placing recycling bins around the shop as well as within our cafe and dining room. We want to see a difference, so we recycle an average of 60% of our grocery store waste.

Composting:

Our compostable to-go containers are fantastic, but they are just the beginning of our composting initiative. We also compost the majority of our food scraps and juicer pulp. Composting is a wonderful habit that we should all adopt to improve the environment.

Environmentally Friendly Building Materials:

We choose architects and contractors who will assist us in building our shop using locally sourced and recycled materials. You'll see that we use recycled materials in our construction, such as the recycled glass we used to decorate the concrete countertops at our Dawson's Market cafe. We look for long-lasting materials that don't just have a "green" picture, but also provide us with a more environmentally friendly way of building. Aside from our construction supplies, we want to be environmentally conscious by keeping our energy meters down while the lights are turned on. We accomplish this by using LED and fluorescent bulbs and fixtures, which use less electricity while providing excellent illumination and emitting less heat. Not only can these bulbs and fixtures save us energy, but they they last for longer than standard bulbs.
