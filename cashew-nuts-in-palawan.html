15. When Grace baked 120 cookies, she put cashew nuts in 40% of them. How many cookies had cashew nuts in them?


Answer:

To find out how many cookies had cashew nuts in them, you can use the following proportion:

(number of cookies with cashew nuts) / (total number of cookies) = (percentage of cookies with cashew nuts) / 100

So, if we let x be the number of cookies with cashew nuts, we can set up the proportion like this:

x / 120 = 40 / 100

To solve for x, we can cross-multiply and get:

x = (40/100) * 120

Simplifying, we get:

x = 48

So, 48 cookies had cashew nuts in them
