17. what is a snipe nose pliers​


Needle-nose pliers (also known as pointy-nose pliers, long-nose pliers, pinch-nose pliers or snipe-nose pliers) are both cutting and holding pliers used by artisans, jewellery designers, electricians, network engineers and other tradesmen to bend, re-position and snip wire.

Answer:

Snipe-nose pliers or also known as "needle-nose pliers" are both cutting and holding pliers used by artisans, jewellery designers, electricians, network engineers and other tradesmen to bend, re-position and snip wire. Their namesake long nose gives excellent control while the cutting edge near the pliers' joint provides "one-tool" convenience.

Snipe nose pliers are great general purpose pliers for bending metal sheet and wire and their tapered jaws narrow to a fine point, making them really useful for closing crimps in awkward to reach places.
