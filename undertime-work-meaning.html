3. Can undertime be offset by over time? Explain?​


Answer:

Undertime not offset by overtime.

Undertime work on any particular day shall not be offset by overtime work on any other day. Permission given to the employee to go on leave on some other day of the week shall not exempt the employer from paying the additional compensation required in this Chapter.

11. Molecules of gases are fatther aprt,thus sound travels in gases a the slowest b the fastest c with undertimed d with varying speed


The closer the molecules of a substance, the faster sound travels.

The hierarchy of distance between molecules, from least to greatest is

Gas < Liquid < Solid

Since in gas, molecules are really far apart, sound travels the slowest in this medium.

The answer is a) the slowest
