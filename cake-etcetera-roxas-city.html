17. Mrs. Roxas used about 3/4 of the 3 5/8 kg of flour for baking a cake. About how many kilograms of flour were left?


Answer:

23/8 OR 2 7/8

Step-by-step explanation:

3 5/8 - 3/4 = n

Make the mixed number into improper fraction which is 29/8

find the LCD of 29/8 and 3/4 (which is 8)

8 ÷ 1 × 29 = 29 ( 29/8 did not change)

8 ÷ 4 × 3 = 6 (6 is the new numerator of 3/4 so it becomes 6/8)

29/8 - 6/8 = 23/8 OR 2 7/8 (simplified)
