17. (With solution)The combined mass of a Bulldog and an angora cat is 48 kg. Bulldog's mass is seven times heavy as the Angora cat. Find the mass of each animal.​


Let x= the mass of the Angora cat and

7x= the mass of the Bulldog

mathematical sentence:

[tex]x + 7x = 48[/tex]

solution:

Find x to get the mass of the cat,

[tex]x + 7x = 48 \\ 8x = 48 \\ \frac{8x}{8} = \frac{48}{8} \\ x = 6[/tex]

Using x=6, find 7x to get the mass of the dog,

[tex]7x \\ = 7(6) \\ = 42[/tex]

checking:

x=6 and 7x=42,

[tex]x + 7x = 48 \\ \\ 6 + 42 = 48 \\ 48 = 48[/tex]

conclusion:

Therefore, the mass of the Angora cat is 6kg and the mass of the Bulldog is 42kg.

24. 1.) Find three consecutive numbers with the sum of 393.2.) The combined mass of a bulldog and an angora cat is 48 kg. Bulldog's mass is seven times heavy as the Angora cat. Find the mass of each animal.Whom can answer this can be brainliest.​


Answer:

1.

Let x - the first number

x+1 - second number

x+2 - third number

[tex](x) + (x + 1) + (x + 2) = 393 \\ 3x + 3 = 393 \\ 3x = 393 - 3 \\ 3x = 390 \\ x = \frac{390}{3} \\ x = 130[/tex]

If x =130, then

x+1=131

x+2=132

Therefore, 130, 131, and 132 are 3 consecutive numbers.

2.

Let x- mass of bulldog

y- mass of angora cat

"The combined mass of a bulldog and an angora cat is 48 kg" ===> x+y=48 (Equation 1)

"Bulldog's mass is seven times heavy as the Angora cat." ===> x=7y (Equation 2)

Now, you have two equations and two unknowns.

Substitute x=7y to corresponding x+y=48.

7y+y=48

8y=48

y=48/8

y=6

To find x, substitute the value of y to either Equation 1 or 2.

For Equation 2,

x=7y

X=7(6)=42.

Therefore, the mass of bulldog is 42 kg and angora cat is 6kg.
