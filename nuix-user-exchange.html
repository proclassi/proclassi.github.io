Answer:

490 rubies


Step-by-step explanation:

10 rubies = 2 sapphires

7 sapphires = 3 emeralds

12 emeralds = 2 diamonds


Find the number of emeralds 7 diamonds can exchange for:

2 diamonds = 12 emeralds

1 diamond = 12 ÷ 2 = 6 emeralds

7 diamonds = 6 x 7 = 42 emeralds


Find the number of sapphires 42 emeralds can exchange for:

3 emeralds = 7 sapphires

1 emerald = 7 ÷ 3 = 7/3 sapphires

42 emeralds = 7/3 x 42 = 98 sapphires


Find the number of rubies 98 sapphires can exchange for:

2 sapphires = 10 rubies

1 sapphire = 10 ÷ 2 = 5 rubies

98 sapphires = 5 x 98 = 490 rubies


Answer: 7 diamonds can exchange for 490 rubies.
