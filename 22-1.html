2. what is the type of property this 22(-16+1/2)=(22)(-16)+(22)(1/2)?​


Answer:

Distributive Property of Multiplication over Addition (DPMA)

Step-by-step explanation:

This property has a long name so I will use its acronym DPMA.

DPMA basically says that if a monomial and binomial is multiplied to each other, do multiplication first before addition.

For example:

2(3+4) = 2(3) + 2(4) = 14

The next one shows the incorrect use of DPMA.

2(3+4) = 2(3) + 4 = 10

This is where most students answer incorrectly because of carelessness, so be careful!

Hope this helps!

18. III. Find the sum of the first 22 natural numbers. (5pts ) Given: a1=1 n=22 a1=1 an=22 Sn=?​


SUM OF AN ARITHMETIC SEQUENCE

Find the sum of the first 22 natural numbers.

[tex]\bold{GIVEN:}[/tex]

[tex]a_1[/tex] = 1 [tex]n[/tex] = 22 [tex]a_n[/tex] = 22 [tex]S_n[/tex] = ?

[tex]\bold{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

Since the first and last term of the sequence are given, we will use the formula [tex]S_n=\frac{n}{2}(a_1+a_n)[/tex] where:

[tex]S_n[/tex] = sum of the first n terms[tex]a_1[/tex] = first term [tex]a_n[/tex] = last term

[tex] \begin{array}{l} \tt S_n = \frac{n}{2} (a_1 + a_n) \\ \\ \tt S_n = \frac{22}{2} (1 + 22) \\ \\ \tt S_n = 11(23) \\ \\ \red{ \boxed{ \tt S_n = 253}} \end{array}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

∴ The sum of the first 22 natural numbers is 253.
