25. Two cards are drawn one after the other with replacement from astandard deck of cards. What is the probability that the first carddrawn is an ace and a second is a face card? *a.4/169b.2/169c.1/169d.3/169​


Answer:

3/169

Step-by-step explanation:

standard deck of cards = 52 cards

number of ace cards = 4 cards (spade, diamond, heart, and club)

number of face cards = 3 x 4 = 12 cards

probability of getting an ace card:

P1 = 4/52 = 1/13

probability of getting a face card:

P2 = 12/52 = 3/13

probability of getting a first card ace and second face card:

P1 x P2 = 1/13 x 3/13

P1 x P2 = 3/169
