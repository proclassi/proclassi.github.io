1. Scaffold Activity (25 pts) () A. Search for the lists of common BIOS Manufacturers for desktops and laptops. Provide a brief description of their origin its latest versions. Give 3 examples only. ​


Answer:

1. American Megatrends Inc. (AMI)
2. Phoenix Technologies
3. Insyde Software

Explanation:

American Megatrends is a Firmware developer that was created in 1985 by S. Shankar Pat Sarma. Before they become the most well known BIOS manufacturer, they manufacture motherboards and BIOSes for Dell. Today, computer company buy their BIOSes like Aorus and Gigabyte and modify them. AMI stopped manufacturing hardware and just focus on Firmware. Their latest BIOS now is currently Aptio.

Phoenix Technologies in the other hand are founded in 1979 by Gerard Moore. They started develop legal BIOS when companies we're being sued by copying the PC BIOS from IBM PC Technical Reference Manual. And then in 1984 they introduced the Phoenix PC ROM BIOS. Today, we see them as the one of the most well known BIOS manufacturer. Their latest BIOS is SecureCore™.

Insyde Software was formed by Jeremy Wang & Jonathan Joseph when they purchased the BIOS assets of SystemSoft Corporation in 1998. Intel presented the business to a new BIOS coding design called EFI (now UEFI) shortly after the initial investment, and the two companies began working jointly on it. And in 2003, InsydeH20 was born and it is still the latest BIOS that they offer to you.
