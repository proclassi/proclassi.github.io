2. describe nagrebcan bauang la union​


Answer:

Describing Nagrebcan, La Union

Nagrebcan is one of the 39 barangays of Bauang, La Union. This barangay has a population of 1,443 as of the latest census, making it the 21st most populated barangay in the town. This barangay is situated adjacent to the Balili River, and this is where you can find the Bauang Bridge.

The majority of the territory of Nagrebcan is a rice field, and most of the population live close to the major roads, like the McArthur Highway and the Naguilian Road. There are also a number of trees northeast of the town, which are mainly fruit bearing trees owned by the residents.

Problem:A parabolic arch has a height of 16m and a width of 28m at the base. If the vertex of the parabola is at the top of the arch, how wide is the arch at the point 11m above its base?

​

Solution:

x² = -4ay

(x, y)

(14, -16)

14² = -4a(-16)

196 = 64a

196/64 = a

a = 3.0625

x² = -4ay

x² = -4(3.0625)y

x² = -12.25y

x² = -12.25(-5)

x² = 61.25

x = √61.25

x = 7.826 m

Width of arch at the pt 11m above the base:

W = 7.826(2)

16. An arch over an entrance is semicircular with an opening that is 5 feet wide. Write an equation that models that arch.


Answer:

If the opening is 5 feet wide, I assume you mean from the center point of the straight edge to the top of the arch. Thus the radius of the arch is 5 feet. Since it is on top, I assume the arch points upward and so the equation is y = √(25-x2)

This equation can be found from the equation of a circle (x-h)2+(y-k)2=r2. The point (h,k) is the center of the circle (here we defined the center to be (0,0)) and r=5 so r2=25. Thus x2+y2=25. Then you solve for y but only use the positive answer since we're talking about the top semicircle.

24. How does the blood in the pulmocutaneous arch differ from that in the higher two aortic arches


Answer:

The skin and lungs receive nearly pure blood from the right atrium through pulmocutaneous arteries. From the skin and lungs, fresh oxygen can be picked up. The blood that passes through the aortic arches is very mixed up, but still has a lot of oxygen in it.
