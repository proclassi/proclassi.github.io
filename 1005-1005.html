335 ÷ 5 = 67 - No remainder! 5 is one of the factors!

67 ÷ 5 = 13.4 - There is a remainder. We can't divide by 5 evenly anymore. Let's try the next prime number

67 ÷ 7 = 9.5714 - This has a remainder. 7 is not a factor.

67 ÷ 11 = 6.0909 - This has a remainder. 11 is not a factor.

67 ÷ 13 = 5.1538 - This has a remainder. 13 is not a factor.

Step-by-step explanation:

Total of mangoes = 1,675

Mangoes each crate = 67

Numbers of crates = 1675 ÷ 67 = 25

Cost of each crate = ₱1,005.00

Total cost:

(multiply the numbers of crate to the cost of each crate)

1,005 × 25 = 25,125

The total cost of mangoes is ₱25,125.00.
