26. Research Problem #1: Suppose the researcher wishes to conduct a study on the effect of lab-lab and seaweed meal as supplemental feeds of 20 milkfish cultured in the fish pond. Lab-lab is the control group because this is the natural food of milkfish and seaweed meal is the experimental group. How will you design the experiment? Design Parameters: Type of Research Design: Experimental units/subjects: Treatment/s: Diagram/Illustration:


Type of Research Design: The study will use a randomized complete block design.

Experimental units/subjects: The experimental units will be 20 milkfish that will be cultured in a fish pond.

Treatment/s: The two treatments will be lab-lab (control group) and seaweed meal (experimental group). The milkfish will be randomly assigned to either the control group or the experimental group. The control group will be fed with lab-lab, which is the natural food of milkfish, while the experimental group will be fed with seaweed meal.
