11. Anthony is 5 years older than Benson. Robert is 3 years younger than Benson. If the sum of their age is 50, how old is each? Solve with solution.


Answer:

Benson = 16Anthony = 21Robert = 13

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be Benson's age.

x + 5 be Anthony's

And x - 3 be Robert's.

[tex]x + (x + 5) + (x - 3) = 50 \\ x + x + 5 + x - 3 = 50 \\ x + x + x = 50 - 5 + 3 \\ 3x = 48 \\ \frac{3x}{3} = \frac{48}{3} \\ x = 16[/tex]

Benson = 16

Anthony = 16 + 5

= 21

Robert = 16 - 3

= 13

16 + 21 + 13 = 50

Henry has 2*X in savings.

Johnson has X - 33 in savings.

Total savings is X + 2*X + X - 33 = 1927

4*X - 33 = 1927
