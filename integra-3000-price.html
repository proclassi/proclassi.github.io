5. 10. You have bought a pair of shoes at Php 2,400 with 30% marked down of price, how much was the original price? A . Php 3,500 B. Php 3000 C. Php 2,500 D. Php 2,000


Answer:

A.Php 3,500

Step-by-step explanation:

If the final price of the shoes after a 30% markdown is Php 2,400, then the original price can be found by dividing the final price by 0.7 (which represents 100% minus the 30% markdown):

Original price = Final price / (1 - markdown percentage)

Original price = Php 2,400 / (1 - 0.30)

Original price = Php 2,400 / 0.70

Original price = Php 3,428.57 (rounded to the nearest cent)

Therefore, the original price of the shoes was Php 3,428.57, which is closest to option A (Php 3,500). Answer: A. Php 3,500.
